Skyline Commercial Complex

Mumbai Completed: 2023 Commercial

The Skyline Commercial Complex features our premium aluminium curtain wall system spanning across 25 floors. The project required precise engineering to accommodate the building's unique curvature while maintaining energy efficiency.

Key Features:

  • Unitized curtain wall system with thermal breaks
  • Custom anodized finish for uniform appearance
  • High-performance glazing for energy efficiency
  • Automated sun shading system

25,000 sq.ft

Duration

8 months

Materials Used

High-grade aluminium alloy
