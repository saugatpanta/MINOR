About Pashupati Aluminium

Leading the way in innovative aluminium solutions since 2010

Our Story

Founded in 2010, Pashupati Aluminium began as a small manufacturing unit with a vision to revolutionize the aluminium industry in India. Today, we've grown into one of the most trusted names in premium aluminium solutions for architectural and construction applications.

Our journey has been marked by continuous innovation, quality craftsmanship, and a commitment to sustainable manufacturing practices. We've successfully completed over 500 projects across residential, commercial, and institutional sectors.

Our Milestones

Key achievements in our journey of excellence

2010

Company Founded

Established our first manufacturing facility in Kathmandu with 20 employees.

2014

First Major Project

Completed the aluminium facade for the prestigious Sky Tower in Mumbai.

2017

Expansion

Opened second manufacturing unit in Kathmandu to serve Lalitpur.

2020

Sustainability Initiative

Implemented eco-friendly manufacturing processes and recycling program.

2023

ISO Certification

Achieved ISO 9001:2023 certification for quality management systems.
